General Requirements

To be considered for admission, applicants must satisfy the following minimum criteria:

  • Completed application form.
  • Certified copy of the last academic report/transcript.
  • Copy of the birth certificate.
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs.
  • Non-refundable application fee of Ksh 1,000.

Simple Admission Process

  1. Step 1: Download & Complete
    Get the application form from the link below or from the school office.
  2. Step 2: Submit Documents
    Submit the completed form and all required documents by the deadline.
  3. Step 3: Interview/Assessment
    Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview or academic assessment.
  4. Step 4: Receive Offer
    Successful applicants receive an offer letter with fee structure details.
